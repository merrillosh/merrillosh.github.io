Sacred Ground sets itself apart from traditional garden products brands with its dedication to eco-conscious indoor plant care. Most dominant brands in the industry produce synthetic fertilizers that cause massive environmental damage, but Sacred Ground exclusively offers natural fertilizers. The brand is aimed at Millenial and Gen-Z consumers who favor mission-driven brands and whose lifestyles are more suited to raising a curated collection of houseplants than caring for large flowerbeds.

To accompany their unique message, Sacred Ground's identity draws a sharp contrast to brands like Scott's and MiracleGro. Their name, Sacred Ground, alludes to the spiritual connection between humans and the natural world as well as the reverence and tenderness with which we care for the plants that share our homes. This is also embodied in the brand's signature hand-drawn illustration style and the recurring motif of open hands. Sacred Ground's symbolism draws from traditional Scandanavian folk art, Mexican Otomi textiles, and religious and mystical elements which capture the authenticity of the brand and its mission. The typography balances the brand's bold color palette and psychedelic wordmark with a clean modern sans-serif font.

It goes without saying that Sacred Ground isn't like anything you've seen in your local Home Depot aisle. Unlike generic plastic bags of toxic fertilizer, Sacred Ground's brand and products represent the celebration and preservation of nature, both indoors and out.
