HERITAGE MEAL KITS
2022

Heritage is a branding concept for a meal kit service in a unique market niche. While competitors focus primarily on their offerings' low price, environmental benefits, health, and variety of global flavors, Heritage emphasizes the taste of their Southern and Traditional American meals and taps into the nostalgia of older consumers.

The food photos which appeaer alongside the original mockups below are collected from Unsplash, Material Kitchen, and the Smithsonian Collections of historical images.


SERVICES

Branding Design, Illustration

VINTAGE CHARM, AUTHENTICITY, AND DISTRESSING

Heritage's branding emulates the charm of a local grocery or deli with origins pre-dating the 1950s. It replicates the kind of variation in typography and layout that might occur over several decades in a family business but maintains unity through the color palette and copywriting tone. The visuals also incorporate the unique distressing of a variety of historical artistic techniques including damaged rubber stamps and chipped paint to elicit nostalgia and convey a personalized, homemade feel.

These style choices were partially inspired by the work of The Family Bros Studio as well as the set decoration work of Annie Atkins.

TYPOGRAPHY & FONT MIXING

Bethany Heck's 2021 article on the value of designing with a mixture of typefaces beyond the generic serif/sans-serif pairing of many modern brands also inspired this exploration. A wide variety of fonts appear in the paper collateral and merchandising mixing period styles with modern imitators and handwritten details and are particularly key to achieving realism and authenticity in Heritage's faux-vintage identity.

ILLUSTRATIONS & ART DIRECTION

The orginal illustrations combine more modern character styles with halftone shading and mild distressing to produce a familiar family who would look as at home on the packaging of a trendy DTC brand as they would in comic section of a 60s newspaper. Beyond hand-drawing, Heritage's imagery is also strengthened through the use of occasional accent photos of prepared meals chosen for the vibrant warm and cool tones of the food.
