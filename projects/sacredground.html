Sacred Ground

Sacred Ground

— Next Gen Plant Care

Sacred Ground is an original branding and packaging design concept for a line of eco-conscious fertilizers. Their products allude to the spiritual connection between humans and the natural world and are aimed at Millenial and Gen-Z consumers whose lifestyles are more suited to raising a curated collection of houseplants than caring for large flowerbeds.
To accompany their unique message, Sacred Ground's identity draws a sharp contrast to brands like Scott's and MiracleGro.

Through their hand-drawn illustrations and symbolism drawn from traditional folk art and religious and mystical sources, the brand celebrates the preservation of nature, both indoors and out.
