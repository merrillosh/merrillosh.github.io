Wing Category Navigation

Wing Delivery App

– Flexible Category Navigation

Inspired by consumers' constant awareness of their locations while navigating physical stores, the new navigation model for shopping from large retailers within the Wing delivery app is discoverable, lightweight, and convenient. It makes jumping between product categories smooth and fluid
while seamlessly allowing users to scroll through product listings.

In user testing, the altered UI reduced shopping time by 17% and the partner who initially requested an update to the navigation system was very pleased with the results.
