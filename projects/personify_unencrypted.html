Personify Health

– Personalized and Personable

Personify is a first-of-its-kind health and wellbeing company. They boast an innovative, holistic approach, offering health benefits administration, engaging wellbeing programs, data-backed analytical tools, and concierge-level service—all on one platform.
Tasked with developing an identity as bold as this new venture, the team at Prophet explored numerous styles centered around Personify's "person first" approach.

The final identity feels empowering, highlights their unique audiences, and stands out among the serious expressions of their competitive set.

BRANDMARK


While I was the first to propose using the brand monogram as the right half of a face, I could never have envisioned how our emotive secondary brandmark would grow to become the ultimate personification of the brand.

Cleverly named "Percy", this mark highlights Personify's human-centricity and adds a delightful spark to consumer touchpoints. Percy's outline also forms the basis of the graphic devices and patterns we developed.



PALETTE & FLEXIBILITY


We developed a color palette around a distinctive yellow to convey warmth and optimism. However, with these vivid tones we also crafted specific guidelines for modulating color usage based on the intenion of a given appplication.

By categorizing communications into those designed to inform, engage, and excite users, we were able to flex this new identity across communications, ensuring that we always set the appropriate tone.

UI, WEB, & MOTION


Because so many of Personify's members regularly connect with the company through digital touchpoints, we spent extra time and care defining the brand's UI components. Unlike many brands, Personify also recognized the importance of a unique and unified approach to motion. Together, we defined three key motion principles: engage with motion, highlight people, and make it snappy.

The final UI elements and motion techniques are best brought to life on the new home page, designed by Prophet and executed by an external development team.

From the Percy mark to the custom pictograms, the web page is the perfect example of how each brand element comes together to create an upbeat, lighthearted, and singularly human experience.

OUTCOMES & RECEPTION


In early 2024, the rebranding was publicly announced and garnered overwhelmingly positive reactions, especially from a key stakeholder audience: the employees of the legacy brands brought together in the new company. Customers and prospects showed interest too, with many reaching out with partnership inquiries.

The new identity garnered more than 6.1 Million impressions driven by 10 original media articles within the first two weeks. It also recieved positive public statements from health economists, industry analysts, and of course, members of the Personify executive team.
