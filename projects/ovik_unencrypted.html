Ovik Health

Ovik Health

– Weaving New Health Narratives

In 2024, Ovik Health began working with Prophet to launch a small campaign as part of their parent company Milliken's broader design refresh. However, what began with some simple web ads and LinkedIn posts would become a beautiful new visual identity for the brand.
Drawing on their unique offering of advanced textiles for wound care, Ovik's identity is harmonious with but distinct from their parent company's. Colorful and joyful, the new look offers a fresh perspective in an industry of clinical visuals and highlights the quality of life their technology enables.

INITIAL CONCEPTS


We initially created two campaign concepts for Ovik. The first uses a glass-like overlay inspired by Ovik's textiles to focus in on moments of touch and care while the second brings in a new graphic device, The Thread, to represent the

interconnected nature of Ovik's innovation and care while nodding to its heritage. To maintain unity with their parent brand, both campaign concepts used a selection of colors from Milliken's broad palette as well as variations of Milliken's typography style.



REFINED DIRECTION


With our client loving the second direction and Thread device, refinements mostly focused on the campaign language and photography selection. Each photo was chosen carefully so as to

accurately reflect Ovik's diverse products and patients. Using sparing AI enhancements, we provided a wide variety of product shots in non-clinical settings and joy-filled everyday contexts.

FROM CAMPAIGN TO IDENTITY


After the campaign launched, Ovik revealed that their executive and design teams had fallen head-over-heels for the new design. From the intentional colorblocking of patient imagery to the delicate Thread device, they loved how the new ads were authentic to their past while setting a unique tone for their future. The client requested that we turn the visual aspects of the campaign into a clearly defined system that could be used as their visual identity going forward.

In response, I provided them a succinct but thorough breakdown of everything from the layout grids to detailed photo editing instructions in the form of the toolkit pages to the right.

It goes without saying that they are absolutely thrilled that what started as an ad campaign has allowed them to grow away from Milliken aesthetically and begin building a clear and unified visual footprint.
