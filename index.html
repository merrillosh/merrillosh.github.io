Merrill O'Shaughnessy

—Product & UX designer. I like to create things that are as charming and playful as they are functional.
For the past two years, I've been studying Human-centered Design and Informatics at Duke University. I intend to graduate with my B.S. in 2020, but right now I'm focused on expanding my design skillset and helping new engineering teams learn to communicate. Before this, I interned at Code Platoon, learning and teaching full-stack web development.
I grew up in the 'burbs of Chicago, make a lot of self deprecating jokes, and I'm determined to explore just about everything in the world of tech and design. I spend my free time drooling over beautiful packaging, clipping illustrations from newspapers, and raising a jungle of indoor plants.
