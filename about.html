    Merrill O'Shaughnessy —

    Multidisciplinary designer. I approach every project with empathy, experimentation, and an eye for the delightfully unusual.

    I'm currently studying Human-centered Design and Informatics* at Duke University and I'll be graduating with my B.A. in December 2022. Right now I'm focused on expanding my design skillset and applying both my passion for big-picture creative strategy and hands-on visual design work as a Creative Director and UX Designer at Pitch Story Lab, a student powered marketing agency. Previously, I interned as a UX Designer at Mastercard, rethinking some of their widest-reaching employee-facing tools and at Wing, shaping customer interactions with their fleet of ultra-fast delivery drones. Outside of design, I also worked at Code Platoon teaching full-stack web development†. However, my real passion lies in concepting and creating unique identities that celebrate—not eliminate—the idiosyncrasies that connect customers to a brand.

    I grew up just outside of Chicago‡, make a lot of self deprecating jokes§, and enjoy getting my hands dirty exploring every step of the design process. I spend my free time drooling over beautiful packaging, clipping illustrations from newspapers, and raising a jungle of indoor plants¶.

    Presently, I'm seeking full-time positions in UX and Brand Identity Design post-graduation in December 2022.
